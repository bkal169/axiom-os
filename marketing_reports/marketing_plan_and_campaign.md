# Axiom OS V3: Master Marketing Plan & Go-To-Market Funnel

## 1. Executive Summary & The "Warm Open" Strategy

Axiom OS is not a mass-market SaaS; it is a premium **Enterprise Spatial OS**. Our launch strategy avoids cheap, broad-spectrum advertising. Instead, we use a highly-targeted "Drop" model (like a luxury apparel brand or an exclusive financial club).

**The "Warm Open" Social Campaign (LinkedIn & Twitter/X Focus):**
We do not run "Sign Up Now" ads. We run "Request Access" campaigns featuring high-fidelity, 15-second screen recordings of the Axiom Copilot in action.

* **Week 1 (Teaser):** "The end of the 12-hour underwrite." (Video: `ZoningAnalyzer` calculating build-out in 3 seconds).
* **Week 2 (Pain Point):** "Is your acquisition team still taking notes on paper?" (Video: The `Field Dashboard` syncing voice memos from an iPad in an empty lot).
* **Week 3 (The Drop):** "Axiom OS V3 is online. 50 Beta Invites Available. Request Access." (Video: 3D Mapbox Terrain with Live WebSocket Pricing).

## 2. The Marketing Funnel Architecture

### Stage 1: Top of Funnel (Awareness via Micropages)

**The 50 Micropage Strategy**
Instead of one massive SEO landing page, we programmatically generate 50 highly specific "Micropages" targeting long-tail intent.

* **Example URLs:**
  * `axiom.co/use-cases/multi-family-zoning-austin`
  * `axiom.co/use-cases/industrial-field-inspections-ipad`
  * `axiom.co/use-cases/costar-argus-alternative-pe-firms`
* **Content:** Each micropage focuses on a single Axiom feature solving a single problem for a specific persona. These drive hyper-focused organic search traffic.

### Stage 2: Middle of Funnel (The E-Book & ROI Calculator)

When a user lands on a Micropage, the Call-to-Action (CTA) is not "Buy." It is:

1. **Download the E-Book:** "Mastering Spatial Intelligence in CRE." (Captures Email/Role/Company Size).
2. **The Interactive ROI Calculator:** A widget where they input their analyst headcount and average deal size. The widget calculates (using Axiom's Phase 8 logic) exactly how much time/money they are losing by not using Axiom.

### Stage 3: Bottom of Funnel (The "Select Beta" & Demo)

* Once captured in the CRM, the prospect is placed in an automated email drip sequence.
* **Email 1:** "Welcome to Axiom. Here is your E-Book."
* **Email 2 (Day 3):** Case Study: The 48-Hour Multi-Family Land Acquisition.
* **Email 3 (Day 7):** "Your firm qualifies for our Q3 Beta. Book a live architecture demo."
* **The Demo:** Conducted by a Managing Partner. We do not show slides; we log into the live Axiom OS V3 environment and underwrite a live property in their specific target city in under 5 minutes.

## 6. The " Spatial Intelligence in CRE" Webinar Setup

### 6.1 The Hook & Structure

We do not host boring "software walkthroughs." We host masterclasses on *Speed-to-Execution*.

* **Title:** The 48-Hour Acquisition: Why Spreadsheets are Killing Your Deal Flow.
* **Target Audience:** Managing Directors, Acquisition Leads, Senior Underwriters.
* **Format:** 45 Minutes (Live Demo + Q&A). No slides.

### 6.2 The Outline

* **Minutes 0-5: The Problem (The Status Quo)**
  * Highlight the ridiculousness of tabbing between CoStar, Argus, Google Earth, and Excel.
  * State the thesis: *The friction between the physical asset and the financial model is where deals die.*
* **Minutes 5-15: The Automation (The Magic Trick)**
  * Live feed: We take an address submitted by the audience in the chat.
  * We plug it into Axiom OS. In under 5 minutes, we show the 3D map, the zoning calculation, the 5-mile comp radius, and the 10-year generated baseline pro-forma.
* **Minutes 15-25: The Field Operations**
  * Switch the screen share to an iPad.
  * Demonstrate the `Field Dashboard`.
  * Show how an acquisition manager taking a voice note on-site instantly syncs to the analyst's dashboard in the office.
* **Minutes 25-35: The ROI & Synergy**
  * Discuss the math. What is the cost of your analyst's time?
  * Discuss the qualitative value: Speed to LOI.
* **Minutes 35-45: Q&A & The Call to Action**
  * "We are capping the V3 Beta at 50 firms. We currently have 32 spots left."
  * Provide the exclusive application link in the chat.
